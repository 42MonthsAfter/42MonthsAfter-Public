THANK YOU FOR SUBSCRIBING!

This is my first public mod, so thanks for taking a look. Configuration files are included, so it's not hard to put these into your server!
I will be adding more shirts weekly or biweekly, and I do take suggestions, and I will also make custom clothing upon request.

PLEASE MAKE SURE TO LOOK AT THE TYPES FILE BEFORE INSERTING INTO YOUR SERVER!!!!!

This mod has been split into two different types due to your requests. 
Anything with the suffix NA will NOT have enhanced attributes, and only inheret the values of vanilla items. 
The original items (the ones without NA at the end) will have the same armor rating as a plate carrier, the same water resistance as a raincoat, and extra insullation for warmth.

SERVER OWNERS PLEASE MAKE SURE YOU LOOK AT THE FILES BEFORE YOU PUT THEM IN!!! DON'T BLAME ME IF YOU SCREW IT UP!!! :)
